Sentinels of the Multiverse

I've made a bunch of homebrew content for the Sentinels of the Multiverse cooperative card game. The premise of that game is pretty simple: the players are a group of heroes working together to defeat a villain. Each hero and villain has a unique deck that represents their personality and abilities, and there are also environment decks that represent locations. This simple framework provides a fun space for creating new decks based on established characters!

All of the decks I've made are part of collections which are summarized below. They can can be played in Tabletop Simulator on Steam through my Workshop mod.
