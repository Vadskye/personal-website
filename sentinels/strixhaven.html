Strixhaven

This collection of decks is based on Strixhaven, which a plane of existence in Magic: the Gathering, by Wizards of the Coast. Strixhaven is a magical university with five core colleges. Each college represents a different magical specialization.

Environments

Lorehold, College of Archaomancy

Prismari, College of Elemental Arts

Quandrix, College of Numeromancy

Silverquill, College of Eloquence

Witherbloom, College of Essence Studies
