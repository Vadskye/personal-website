Ravnica

This collection of decks is based on Ravnica, which a plane of existence in Magic: the Gathering, by Wizards of the Coast. Ravnica is a vast city ruled by ten guilds, each of which represents a particular combination of two of the five colors in Magic. The personality of each guild is designed to reflect the combination of the philosophies of those two colors. In Sentinels-land, I've translated that into one environment deck for each guild.
